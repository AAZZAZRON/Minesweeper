Minesweeper
The basic idea to playing Minesweeper is that you attempt to find the bombs in a grid.

The grid is completely filled with unknown squares. Some contain empty space, and some contain bombs. Your goal is to reveal all the empty space without hitting a bomb.

To help you solve this puzzle, Numbers in squares next to unrevealed squares will indicate how many bombs are adjacent to it (corners included).

For example, if an empty square has a 2 in it, there are 2 bombs next to it.

To reveal a space you believe doesn't contain a bomb, click on that square with the LEFT mouse button.

To mark a space you believe contains a bomb, click using your RIGHT mouse button.

You win if you reveal all the empty spaces without clicking on a bomb.

Good luck!
